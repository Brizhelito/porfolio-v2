## Technical Summary

Administrative dashboard for a POS system with multi-branch management. Client-server architecture with REST API, Redis caching for high-frequency reports, and PostgreSQL as the source of truth.

## Lab Notes

<LabNotes>
  <LabNotes.Note date="2023-07-20" topic="Caching strategy">
    **Problem**: Sales reports took 3+ seconds with 6 months of data.

    **Decision**: Redis with event-based invalidation (new sale → invalidate daily report cache). Latency reduced to 50ms.
  </LabNotes.Note>
</LabNotes>
