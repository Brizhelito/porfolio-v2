## Technical Summary

Educational platform for teaching linear algebra with emphasis on matrices. Interactive visual editor, collaborative mode with real-time presence via Socket.io, and automatic exercise grading.

## Lab Notes

<LabNotes>
  <LabNotes.Note date="2023-04-15" topic="Math rendering">
    **Decision**: KaTeX over MathJax. 10x faster rendering, sufficient for our LaTeX subset. MathJax added as fallback for complex expressions.
  </LabNotes.Note>

  <LabNotes.Note date="2023-08-20" topic="Real-time collaboration">
    **Problem**: Multiple users editing the same matrix simultaneously.

    **Decision**: Simplified Operational Transform for matrix operations (single cell). Conflict resolution by cell position — two users can edit different cells without conflict.
  </LabNotes.Note>
</LabNotes>
