## Technical Summary

Tool developed for a real academic environment, capable of solving advanced mathematical theorems (Gauss, Gauss-Jordan, determinants, inverse matrix). Delegates algorithmic calculations to the Java backend to ensure numerical precision, keeping the client lightweight with HTML, SCSS, and vanilla JavaScript.
